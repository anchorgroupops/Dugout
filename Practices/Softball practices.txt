2/4/2026
Objectives: Build arm strength, throwing mechanics, baserunning speed/conditioning, outfield positioning/communication, fielding strategy, force outs, low accurate throws. Emphasize fun, safety, rotation, proper form.
1. Stretch/Warmup (15 min)
   * Dynamic stretches: Arm circles (10 forward/backward each arm), shoulder rolls, wrist rotations.
   * Leg stretches:Butterflies, quad pulls, hamstring reaches.
   * Light jog: 1 lap around the field.
   * Objective: Increase flexibility, prevent injury, prepare body for activity.
2. Kneel Throw (20 min)
   * Line up in outfield pairs, 15-20 ft apart.
   * Kneel on glove-side knee, play catch using upper body only.
   * Rotate upper bodies for proper mechanics.
   * Game: Pairs compete to catch most in a row.
   * Objective: Improve arm strength, throwing motion.
3. 4-3-2-1 (20 min, focus on running THROUGH 1st)
   * Line up behind home plate.
   * All run 4x to 1st, 3x to 2nd, twice to 3rd, once all around.
   * Game: Miss base or slow down, team restarts.
   * Objective: Baserunning conditioning, speed, proper path through 1st.
4. Outfield Angles (25 min)
   * Equal groups in left/center field. One Coach on 2nd (cutoff) and one coach at home.
   * Hit hard groundball between LF and CF.
   * LF takes angle to ball, CF backs up if missed.  Throw to cutoff.  
   * LF switches to CF.  CF switches to LF.
   * Object: Prevent ball from passing both, and using a cutoff.
   * Key: Backup primary catcher and use cutoff
   * Objective: Cut off hits, communication, positioning.
5. Roll the Ball (Modified, 25 min)
   * Half team runners at home, half fielders in infield positions. A coach in outfield.
   * Runner at bat: Coach pitches, runner hits, drops bat properly, runs THROUGH 1st. Next batter up.
   * Fielders rush ball to 1st for out.
   * Switch teams half way through.
   * Key: Ball stays infield for play; defense anywhere.
   * Game: Play like real game, runners advance/score.
   * Objective: Baserunning, fielding, strategy, proper bat drop and run path.
6. Force Out (20 min)
   * Groups: 1/3 shortstop, 1/3 2nd base, 1/3 running at 1st.
   * Hit to 2nd baseman or shortstop; runner to 2nd.
   * If to shortstop, 2nd catches throw at base (vice-versa).
   * Rotate after everyone twice.
   * Key: Catch safely away from sliding runner.
   * Game: Fielders vs. runners; group with most wins.
   * Objective: Get lead runner out at 2nd, safe fielding.
7. Strike at Home (Fun Ending Game, 25 min)
   * All to 2nd base.
   * Bucket on home plate.
   * Each throws 3 balls to hit bucket.
   * If you hit the bucket, you advance to the next round.  Last player standing wins.
   * Move forward/back, and have tie-breaker if needed.
   * Objective: Accurate low throws, fun competition.

2. 4-3-2-1 (15 min, focus on running THROUGH 1st)

* All run 4x to 1st, 3x to 2nd, twice to 3rd, once all around.
* Game: Miss base or slow down, team restarts.
* Objective: Baserunning conditioning, speed, proper path through 1st.
3. Pepper Drill (15 min)
* Line them up: 3-5 fielders side-by-side, facing one batter 12-15 feet away.
* Fielders throw an easy pitch that batter can hit (soft balls).
* Batter tries to hit soft ground ball back to fielders who field ball and pitch again.
* Key: Emphasize soft swings and ground balls.
* Objective: Hand-eye coordination, bat control, and intentional energy dampening.
4. Soft-Toss (15 min)
* Line them up: One player at plate. Other players fielding.
* Player stands ready to hit with "tosser" facing 4-5 feet away.
* Softly toss balls so that they begin at top of strike zone and fall towards front of plate. Batter hits ball on downward arc.
* Game: Let batter run to 1st on 10th hit.
5. Water Break (5 min)
6. Cutoffs (15 min)
* Line them up: Shortstop and 2nd baseman. Remaining players in the 3 outfield positions.
* Hit ball to outfielder in left. Shortstop runs out halfway between outfielder and base with hands up, calling for ball.
* Outfielder throws to shortstop, (cutoff man), who throws it in to 2nd base.
* Key: Balls hit to left side of field are shortstop's cutoff. Balls hit to right side belong to 2nd baseman.
7. Pickle Drill (20 min)
* Line them up: Place players at SS, 3rd, LF and CF. Remaining players are baserunners.
* Runner rounds 2nd and goes halfway to 3rd. Coach throws ball from LF to 3rd. Runner is now in a "Pickle".
* Fielders try getting runner out with fewest throws necessary.
* Game: Also play between 1st/2nd/3rd/home. Points for safe/out.
8. Water Break (5 min)
9. Live Situations (15 min)
* Line them up: Assign player to each defensive position. Remaining players run bases.
* Hit balls various places on field and play as if live game.
* Rotate runners to fielding positions and vice-versa.
* Before each hit, call out situation, (i.e."runner on 2nd, one out.")
10. Beat 'Em Home (10 min)
* Line them up: One 3rd baseman, one SS, one LF. Remainder take turns running at 2nd.
* Coach hits fly ball to left fielder. Runner at 2nd gets off base a few steps as ball is in air.
* If ball is caught, runner re-touches base and goes to 3rd. Ball not caught, runner goes straight to 3rd.
* Fielder throws to 3rd, trying to get runner out.
* Game: Keep track of runs scored and outs made.
