Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

The Sharks Softball Dashboard is a private coaching tool used exclusively by the coaching staff and authorized personnel of The Sharks, a Palm Coast Little League 12U Girls Softball team in Palm Coast, Florida. This dashboard is not a public service and is not intended for general public use.

Data We Process

This dashboard processes game statistics exported from GameChanger, a third-party sports statistics platform used by Palm Coast Little League. Processed data includes:

  • Player batting averages, on-base percentages, and slugging percentages
  • Fielding statistics and game performance metrics
  • Game outcomes, scores, and season records
  • Player first names and jersey numbers for identification

No sensitive personal information beyond player first names and jersey numbers is stored or displayed. No contact information, home addresses, or financial data is collected or stored by this dashboard.

Data Storage

All data is stored on a private server located in Palm Coast, Florida, operated by the coaching staff. Data is not shared with any third parties and is used solely for coaching analysis and game preparation for The Sharks softball team.

Cookies and Local Storage

This dashboard uses no third-party tracking cookies and no analytics platforms. Browser localStorage is used solely to store user interface preferences, such as player availability settings and UI state between sessions. No personally identifiable information is stored in localStorage.

Third-Party Services

Specific features of this dashboard use the following third-party services: the Spotify API for walk-up music playback (subject to Spotify’s own privacy policy), and AI inference services for lineup optimization and practice plan generation. No player data is transmitted to these services beyond what is strictly necessary for their specific function.

Your Rights

As this is a private team tool restricted to authorized coaching personnel, access control is managed entirely by the coaching staff. If you have questions about data handling or wish to request information about data stored, please contact the head coach through Palm Coast Little League’s internal communication channels.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in how the dashboard operates. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
