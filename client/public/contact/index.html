Contact

The Sharks Softball Dashboard is a private coaching tool for the Palm Coast Little League 12U Girls Softball team in Palm Coast, Florida. Access is restricted to authorized coaching personnel.

Palm Coast Little League

For questions about the Palm Coast Little League organization, game schedules, team registration, or season information, please contact PCLL directly through their official website or registration platform. The league administers youth baseball and softball for players in the Palm Coast, Florida area.

Dashboard Access

This dashboard is maintained by the coaching staff of The Sharks. If you are a coach or authorized team member who needs access, please contact the head coach through the league’s internal communication channels.

Data and Privacy

For questions about how this dashboard processes and stores player statistics, please review the Privacy Policy. All player statistics are sourced from GameChanger, the official sports statistics platform used by Palm Coast Little League, and are processed solely for internal coaching purposes.
